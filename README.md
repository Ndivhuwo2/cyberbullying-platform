# Cyberbullying Documentation Platform

A safe and private web application that helps victims of cyberbullying 
document, track, and report incidents in a structured and credible way.

---

## The Problem

Cyberbullying is often dismissed or ignored because victims struggle to 
present their experiences clearly. Screenshots get lost. Dates are forgotten. 
Without organised evidence, it becomes difficult to report incidents to 
schools, employers, or authorities in a way that is taken seriously.

---

## The Solution

This platform gives victims a private space to:

- Create a case for a cyberbullying situation they are experiencing
- Log individual incidents with the platform, description, and date it occurred
- Upload evidence such as screenshots, images, and voice notes
- Generate a formal PDF report of everything recorded — ready to hand to 
  schools, employers, or law enforcement
- Stay completely anonymous if they are not comfortable creating an account

---

## Who It Is For

- Students experiencing online harassment
- Employees dealing with workplace cyberbullying
- Parents documenting incidents on behalf of their children
- Anyone who needs a safe, private space to record what is happening to them

---

## Privacy First

Your data belongs to you. Anonymous sessions are fully supported — 
no email address or personal information is required to use the platform. 
All uploaded evidence is stored securely with integrity verification 
to ensure nothing can be tampered with.
